# Vision

## Ultimate goal

Bao wants to grow into a product leader who can connect strategy, engineering depth, and execution discipline without losing practical understanding of how systems are built.

## Vision he believes in

Bao believes the most meaningful products are the ones that create tangible value, not just technical novelty. He is especially motivated by products in fintech and AI where technical systems can unlock real commercial or operational impact.

## Environment Bao wants to work in

- High-ownership teams where people can make decisions and move quickly.
- Strong product cultures where strategy, execution, and business context are tightly connected.
- Teams with direct communication, low politics, and clear accountability.
- Environments that value both sharp thinking and the ability to ship.
- Workplaces where technical depth is respected, but not disconnected from users or business outcomes.
