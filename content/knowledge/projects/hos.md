# Heartofshow

- Project type: Personal project
- Timeline: 2022
- Role: Technical Partner

## Problem

There's no digitalize platform/process in Vietnam for booking artist, performer. It prevent small venue/event from finding a suitable talent for their event and new talent to find the jobs

## Solution

Bao create a booking platform which connect and match talent finder and talent themselve. Also it took care of other legal process like tax, contract through digital and realiable process.
