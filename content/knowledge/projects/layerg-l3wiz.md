# LayerG and L3Wiz

- Project type: Product leadership / platform delivery
- Timeline: 2023 to 2025
- Role: Product owner and engineering leader

## Problem

Businesses in retail, F&B, and e-commerce needed more practical ways to bring Web2 use cases into tokenized loyalty, reward, and digital asset flows without forcing deeply technical adoption paths.

## Solution

Bao owned product direction and led engineering delivery for two products: LayerG, a DePIN-based gaming ecosystem, and L3Wiz, a no-code Web3 loyalty platform. The work focused on turning business goals into real products, roadmap decisions, technical delivery, and commercially useful decentralized foundations.

## Contributions

- Managed planning, roadmap, budget, stakeholder alignment, and end-to-end delivery.
- Led product vision and technical execution across both platforms.
- Helped translate traditional Web2 use cases into tokenized loyalty and reward systems.
- Worked on financial mechanism and token economics thinking for decentralized networks.

## Technical stack

- Node.js
- Golang
- PostgreSQL
- Redis
- Microservices
- Distributed systems

## Impact

- Helped create practical foundations for decentralized finance related product flows.
- Contributed to a technical solution that was transferred for more than $50k alongside early adoption.
- Demonstrated Bao's ability to connect architecture, product direction, and stakeholder management.
