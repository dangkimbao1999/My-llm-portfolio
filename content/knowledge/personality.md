# Personality

- Personality type: ENFJ-A

## How Bao tends to show up

Bao tends to be people-oriented, energetic, and comfortable taking the lead when a team needs alignment or momentum. He usually likes turning broad ideas into a shared direction and then pushing execution forward.

## Working personality

- Friendly and expressive in conversation, but serious about ownership and delivery.
- Comfortable connecting business context, product goals, and engineering tradeoffs.
- Often acts as a connector between teams rather than staying only inside a single technical lane.
- Prefers clear decisions and visible progress over slow ambiguity.

## What this means in practice

Bao works best in environments where communication is direct, people care about building together, and initiative is appreciated. He tends to bring energy into collaboration, especially when a project needs structure, momentum, and clearer direction.
